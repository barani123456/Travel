Emergency Travel Assistance

Think of your emergency assistance provider as a concierge to your travel insurance policy benefits. Emergency Travel Assistance (or ETA) is provided by the expert team available to help you when you have your medical travel insurance in place. ETA coverage is available for the insured duration of your trip out-of-province or outside of Canada The ETA team of experts is multi-lingual (helpful when you don’t speak the language where you are) and is networked throughout the world to service providers (so you don’t have to worry about finding help when you are not familiar with the area you are in). If you have an unexpected injury or medical condition, your ETA will refer you to a local doctor, dentist, pharmacist or other appropriate medical facility. Then they maintain contact with you, and continue to monitor your care and the services you are receiving. They will help manage—and pay for—your eligible medical expenses. .
